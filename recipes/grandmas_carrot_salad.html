Grandma's Carrot Salad

Carrot salad is a delightful and nutritious dish that celebrates the vibrant flavors and crunchy texture of fresh carrots. It begins with crisp and grated carrots as the star ingredient. The carrots are usually combined with an array of complementary ingredients like raisins, sliced almonds, or chopped parsley, which add contrasting textures and flavors. The salad may also feature a light and tangy dressing made from ingredients such as lemon juice, honey, olive oil, and Dijon mustard, which enhances the natural sweetness of the carrots. The result is a refreshing and colorful salad that offers a delightful balance of sweet and tangy notes, while providing a satisfying crunch. Carrot salad is not only visually appealing but also packed with essential nutrients, making it a perfect choice for those seeking a healthy and delicious option.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound shredded carrots
  • 1 ¼ cups raisins
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons mayonnaise, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Mix shredded carrots and raisins together in a large serving bowl.
  2. Whisk mayonnaise, lemon juice, and salt in a small bowl until smooth. Pour over carrot mixture; stir until carrots and raisins are completely coated. Refrigerate until chilled, at least 30 minutes.
